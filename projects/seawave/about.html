About

Seawave Transit provides the freshest and
best quality premium seafood to
wholesales suppliers and vendors

'SEAWAVE TRANSIT' brand is synonymous internationally with Quality Seafood. Our motto of 'Customer
Satisfaction First' has inspired us to earn the reputation that our brand carry today in the market. These
seafoods are fresh and shipped overnight to us within 24 hours of being caught before sending it to our
processing plant for deep freezing to preserved its freshness and quality. We customize the packing to
our client needs as well. We are currently supplying to wholesales supplier and even to direct vendors in
the popular hawker centers like Newton Circus , East Coast Lagoon and many more restaurants and hotels.
